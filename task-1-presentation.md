I created my Digital Literacy Awareness infographic using Microsoft PowerPoint. I chose this tool because it is easy to use and allows me to organize content clearly
with text boxes and simple design elements. My design focuses on explaining the concept of digital literacy and why it is important for students. It includes key
sections such as the meaning of digital literacy, useful digital tools like Google Docs and Canva, safe internet practices, professional online presence, and proper
email etiquette. The aim of the design is to present important information in a simple, structured, and visually understandable format so that students can quickly
learn essential digital skills.

One thing I found slightly difficult was deciding how to present a lot of information on a single slide without making it look crowded. It was interesting to learn
how to balance content and design by keeping the text short and using headings to improve readability. This task helped me understand how visual presentation plays
an important role in effective communication.
